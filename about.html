Seelogama Village

Belihuloya Seelogama Community Village is a hidden gem nestled in the heart of Sri Lanka, offering a perfect blend of nature, culture, and adventure. Located in the breathtaking highlands, this village provides a unique opportunity for travelers to experience authentic Sri Lankan rural life while being surrounded by lush greenery, waterfalls, and mountains.
